This chicken Alfredo is a warm, creamy pasta dish featuring tender strips of pan-fried chicken served over soft fettuccine noodles. The pasta is coated in a smooth, buttery Alfredo sauce made with cream and Parmesan, giving it a rich, slightly garlicky flavour. Every bite is soft, savoury, and comforting, with the chicken adding a hearty, well-seasoned contrast to the silky sauce. A light sprinkle of herbs and cheese finishes it off, making it simple, filling, and full of flavour.

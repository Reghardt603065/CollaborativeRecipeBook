## Homemade Pizza

## Ingredients
Dough:
2 ½ cups flour
1 cup warm water
2 tsp instant yeast
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp olive oil
Sauce:
½ cup tomato paste or pizza sauce
1 tsp dried oreganos
1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
Salt and pepper to taste
Toppings (choose what you like):
Grated mozzarella cheese
Sliced chicken, pepperoni, or beef strips
Onion, green pepper, mushrooms
Extra herbs (optional)

## Instructions
1. Make the dough
In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
Add flour, salt, and olive oil.
Mix and knead for about 8–10 minutes until smooth.
Cover and let it rise for 1 hour (until it doubles in size).
2. Prepare the sauce
Mix tomato paste with oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Stir until smooth and set aside.
3. Roll out the dough
Punch down the dough after it has risen.
Roll it into a circle or rectangle on a floured surface.
Place it on a greased baking tray.
4. Add toppings
Spread the sauce evenly over the dough.
Add a layer of cheese.
Add your chosen toppings.
Finish with a little extra cheese on top.
5. Bake the pizza
Preheat oven to 220°C.
Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted and bubbly.
6. Serve
Let it cool for 2–3 minutes.
Slice and enjoy while hot.

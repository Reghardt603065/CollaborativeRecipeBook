# Chicken Alfredo Pasta

## Description
A creamy homemade chicken alfredo pasta recipe that is quick and easy to prepare.

## Ingredients
2 chicken breasts
250g fettuccine pasta
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp olive oil
2–3 garlic cloves (minced)
1 ½ cups fresh cream
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and black pepper (to taste)
Optional: parsley for garnish

## Instructions
1. Cook the pasta
Boil water in a large pot.
Add salt, then cook fettuccine until al dente (firm but cooked).
Drain and set aside (keep a little pasta water for later if needed).
2. Cook the chicken
Slice chicken breasts into strips.
Season with salt and black pepper.
Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
Fry chicken until golden brown and fully cooked (about 5–7 minutes).
Remove and set aside.
3. Make the Alfredo sauce
In the same pan, melt butter.
Add minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds (don’t burn it).
Pour in fresh cream and stir gently.
Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.
4. Add cheese
Slowly add Parmesan cheese while stirring.
Keep stirring until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
5. Combine everything
Add cooked pasta into the sauce.
Toss until fully coated.
Add the cooked chicken back in and mix well.
6. Serve
Plate the pasta.
Sprinkle extra Parmesan and parsley on top if you like.
Serve warm.

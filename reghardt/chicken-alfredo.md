# Chicken Alfredo Pasta

## Description
A creamy homemade chicken alfredo pasta recipe that is quick and easy to prepare.

## Ingredients
- 250g fettuccine pasta
- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 cup fresh cream
- 1 cup parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil the pasta until soft.
2. Season and cook the chicken in a pan.
3. Melt butter and add garlic.
4. Add cream and parmesan cheese.
5. Mix pasta into the sauce.
6. Slice chicken and place on top.
7. Serve hot.
